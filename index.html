Our Story

A Feast Rooted
in Tradition

Ghana — once known as the Gold Coast — is a land rich in flavour, rhythm, and warmth. This event brings that spirit right here to Coventry.

Expect authentic Ghanaian dishes made with love, highlife and Afrobeats filling the air, and games that bring everyone together. Whether you're Ghanaian by birth or by heart, you're welcome at this table.

Your £2.50 entry fee goes straight to your first drink or snack — so you arrive, relax, and get stuck in immediately.

Kelewele

Spicy fried plantain cubes seasoned with ginger, chilli and aromatic spices. A beloved Ghanaian street snack.

Spring Rolls

Crispy golden rolls packed with a savoury filling — the perfect finger food to enjoy between music and games.

Meat Pie

Flaky, golden-baked pastry filled with a well-seasoned meat mixture. A West African party essential.

Coated Groundnut

Crunchy peanuts coated in a light, crispy shell. Great for snacking throughout the evening.
